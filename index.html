Single Class Program

Venice Cooking Class

Wine tours in the area near Venice

Our Veneto Cooking Classes is located near Venice and we offer our guests interesting wine tours in the area around Venice. For more than 10 years, for lovers of Italian food, we have been offering a unique opportunity to participate in small groups in a hands-on cooking experience in the peace of a private historic Venetian Villa of the 16th century.

Meeting point is in Piazzale Roma - Venice, or at the Airport if you aren't already staying at a Hotel.

The local women of Veneto will share Countess Maria Giustiniani's cooking philosopy, her traditional techniques, special ingredients, and family recipes. And you'll enjoy your creation "a tavola" for lunch, together with the best Italian Wines of the region.

One Week
Cooking Vacation

An unfergettable cooking vacation at the Villa. Full board accommodation, cooking lessons in the morning, visit to the traditional Fish Market in Chioggia and wine tours and salami or cheese tasting available in the afternoon.
